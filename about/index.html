Our Story

Affordable Service of Ardmore, Inc. has been serving the RV community for over two decades. What started as a commitment to providing honest, reliable RV repair has grown into one of the most trusted names in Southern Oklahoma.

We know all types of RVs. Bumper pulls, 5th wheels, and motorhomes. When you need mobile repair at your campsite or need to bring your RV to our fully-equipped onsite shop, we'll be there.

From AC and furnace repairs to electrical systems, slide-outs, plumbing, and leveling jacks. We take pride in our workmanship, customer service, and clear communication every step of the way.
